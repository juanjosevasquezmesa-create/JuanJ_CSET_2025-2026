Sun Producer

Sun Producers provide the player the battle currency "Sun". These are integral to the early game and should be preserved but not protected at all cost. Often it is best to place these plants in front of the more expensive attacking plants.

Slow-Down

Slow-Down plants can range from plants that completely stop the zombies to thoes that reduce the progress of zombies. These plants are often put in the direct front of the lawn usually in front of the sun producers and the slow down plants that are often contact based effects are palced on Zombies to have their effects occur.

Attacking plants

These plants are interal to the defense attacking plants can range from instant activating plants to constanst damage dealers. These plants are often the most expensive and for that reason are palced at teh far right back of the lawn to have the protections of the slowdowns and sun producers. These plants should be picked to combat the zombies that are in the levels.
