The Strawhat Crew!

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a rubber-powered pirate who aims to become the next Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure, One Piece, left by the previous Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. To achieve his dream, Luffy gathers a diverse crew of the Straw Hat Pirates, embarking on an epic journey across the dangerous Grand Line, battling powerful enemies, and uncovering the world's hidden history along the way.
